Use Guide

Four simple steps to record attendance.

Easy setup

Walk into your class room as you do normally.

On-the-go

ClassBeacon monitors for iBeacon signals.

Notifies

On entering a room iBeacon triggers an alert prompt to confirm attendance.

Simplicity

Swipe to confirm, Congrats you have now recorded your attendance.
